## 2026-06-08 - Add ARIA labels and tooltips to icon-only buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons lacking `title` and `aria-label` attributes are a common accessibility gap in this app's components, hindering usability for screen reader users and those who rely on tooltips for context.
**Action:** Always verify icon-only buttons include `title` and `aria-label` attributes to ensure both visual context (tooltips) and screen reader accessibility are maintained.
